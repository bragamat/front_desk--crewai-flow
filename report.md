# Mr. Olympia 2025 Report

## Overview
The 2025 Mr. Olympia competition took place during the weekend of October 6-8, 2025. This year's event was marked by an exceptionally strong lineup of competitors, showcasing some of the most impressive performances witnessed in recent years. The competition included two primary categories: the Open category and the Classic Physique category.

## Open Category Results

### Winner: Derek Lunsford
Derek Lunsford emerged as the champion of the Open category, securing his victory amid fierce competition. Lunsford's success at this prestigious event has firmly established him as a leading figure in the bodybuilding world. His exceptional physique and conditioning earned him not only the coveted title but also a substantial prize of **$600,000**.

Finishing in a close second place was Hadi Choopan, who displayed remarkable aesthetics and muscularity throughout the competition. His performance earned him a respectable prize of **$200,000**, reflecting the high stakes and competitive nature of the event.
